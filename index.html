03

Models of Partnership

Three ways to structure the relationship, by how deep the alignment goes. Each is viable on its own; they can also combine.

Path A

Studio Partner

FlumenX delivers the foundation and build on a project basis, with an optional retainer. Clean, immediate, and the simplest way to begin.

Project + retainer
Path B · Recommended

Platform Partner

FlumenX builds, owns and operates the technology estate, licensed to the chamber per chapter and per member. The institution always runs on a living, supported platform; cost scales with growth.

Build + per-chapter licence
Path C

Founding Partner

For the deepest alignment — a founding stake in the operating company in exchange for system design and execution leadership across the build. Structured directly between principals.

Equity · by discussion
On the technology. The platform is the operating spine of the institution — the system every chapter, in every country, runs on. We recommend it is built and maintained as a living product (Path B) rather than handed over and left to age, so the chamber always has a supported, evolving backbone as it scales worldwide.
